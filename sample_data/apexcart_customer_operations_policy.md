# ApexCart Customer Operations Policy

Version: 2.1
Effective Date: January 2026
Document Type: Sample enterprise policy corpus for RAG evaluation

## 1. Refund Eligibility

Customers may request a refund for physical products within 14 calendar days of delivery. The product must be unused, returned with original packaging, and include all accessories shipped with the order. Refund requests submitted after 14 calendar days are not eligible unless the delay was caused by a verified shipping or delivery issue.

Digital service purchases may be refunded within 7 calendar days of purchase only if the service has not been activated or consumed. Once a digital service has been activated, downloaded, or used to generate customer-specific output, it is not eligible for refund.

Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the refund is caused by an ApexCart fulfillment error, duplicate shipment, or incorrect item sent by ApexCart. Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days after the returned item passes inspection.

## 2. Subscription Cancellation and Renewal

Monthly subscription cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing cycle. Customers retain access to subscription benefits until the billing cycle ends. ApexCart does not provide prorated refunds for unused days in a monthly subscription period.

Annual subscription downgrades take effect on the next renewal date. Customers may cancel auto-renewal at any time before the renewal date. If a renewal payment fails, ApexCart will retry the payment up to 3 times within 7 calendar days before suspending subscription benefits.

Subscription cancellation does not automatically delete the customer account. Customers who want account deletion must submit a separate account deletion request.

## 3. Shipping Timelines and Address Changes

Standard shipping usually takes 4 to 6 business days after order dispatch. Express shipping usually takes 2 business days after order dispatch. Remote delivery locations may require up to 8 business days after dispatch.

Customers may request a shipping address change only before the courier pickup is completed. After courier pickup, ApexCart cannot guarantee address changes and customers must coordinate directly with the courier when available.

If an order is marked as delivered but the customer did not receive it, the customer must contact support within 3 calendar days of the delivery timestamp. ApexCart may require delivery proof, courier confirmation, or a signed non-receipt declaration before taking further action.

## 4. Damaged, Defective, or Missing Items

Customers must report damaged or defective products within 48 hours of delivery. The report must include the order ID, clear photos of the damaged product, photos of the outer packaging, and a short description of the issue.

ApexCart may offer a replacement, repair, refund, or store credit after inspection. The final resolution depends on product availability, inspection results, and warranty eligibility.

Missing accessories must be reported within 5 calendar days of delivery. If the missing accessory is confirmed through warehouse records, ApexCart will ship the missing accessory without additional shipping charges.

## 5. Warranty Coverage

ApexCart-branded hardware accessories include a 6-month limited warranty against manufacturing defects. The warranty period begins on the delivery date listed in the order record.

The warranty does not cover water damage, accidental drops, misuse, normal wear and tear, cosmetic scratches, unauthorized repairs, or damage caused by third-party chargers and cables.

Warranty claims require the original invoice, product serial number when available, and photos or videos showing the defect. ApexCart may reject warranty claims if the product has been modified, opened, or repaired by an unauthorized service provider.

## 6. Billing Disputes and Duplicate Charges

Customers must report billing disputes within 30 calendar days of the transaction date. ApexCart will review payment logs, invoice records, payment gateway status, and order fulfillment status before deciding the outcome.

Verified duplicate charges are reversed or refunded within 5 business days. If only one charge was captured and another transaction was only an authorization hold, the customer must wait for the issuing bank to release the hold.

If a customer files a chargeback before contacting ApexCart support, the related account may be temporarily paused until the payment dispute is resolved. ApexCart does not issue wallet credits for chargeback cases.

## 7. Account Deletion and Data Retention

Customers may request account deletion from the account settings page or by contacting support. Account deletion is processed within 30 calendar days after identity verification is completed.

Before deletion, customers should download invoices, order history, and warranty records. Once deletion is completed, ApexCart support cannot restore the account, saved addresses, reward history, or support conversations.

ApexCart may retain invoices, tax records, fraud review logs, chargeback evidence, and compliance records for up to 7 years where required by law or internal risk controls.

## 8. Privacy and Customer Data Access

Customers may request a copy of their personal data once every 90 calendar days. Data exports include profile information, order history, support ticket metadata, saved addresses, and communication preferences.

Data export requests are usually completed within 15 business days after identity verification. ApexCart may reject repeated, abusive, or unverifiable data export requests.

Customer support agents may access customer order information only when handling refunds, shipping issues, billing disputes, warranty claims, fraud checks, or account deletion requests.

## 9. Fraud Review and Order Holds

ApexCart may place orders under fraud review when payment risk signals are detected. Common triggers include high-value orders, mismatched billing and shipping information, repeated failed payment attempts, unusual device activity, and multiple accounts using the same payment method.

Fraud review may delay order dispatch by up to 48 hours. During review, ApexCart may ask the customer to verify billing details, identity, or payment ownership.

If verification is not completed within the requested time, ApexCart may cancel the order and refund the captured payment. Fraud review decisions are final when required by payment risk controls.

## 10. Support Response and Escalation

Standard support tickets receive a first response within 24 business hours on weekdays. Priority support tickets receive a first response within 8 business hours.

Customers may request escalation if an issue remains unresolved after 3 business days from the first support response. Escalated tickets are reviewed by a senior support specialist or operations lead.

Urgent security issues, suspected account takeover, and unauthorized payment activity are reviewed on the same business day when reported through the security support channel.

## 11. Policy Exclusions

This policy does not describe employee benefits, vendor contracts, tax advice, medical advice, legal advice, travel bookings, insurance products, or custom enterprise service-level agreements.

Customers should contact the relevant third-party provider for issues involving banks, courier partners, payment cards, device manufacturers, or marketplace sellers that are not fulfilled directly by ApexCart.
